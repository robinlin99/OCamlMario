Gameplay Directions

'a' and 'd' move left and right respectively. 
Mouse button jumps. A higher jump can be achieved by holding the mouse 
  button longer. 
To fire fireballs while in Fire Mario form, press the shift key in 
  conjunction with any of 'w' 'a' 's' 'd' or 'e'
Jump on enemies to kill them. 
'q' to quit, death and winning auto-quit the game. 
Die by running into enemies or falling off the map.
Win by reaching the end of the level.
